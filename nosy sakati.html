NOSY SAKATIA

description:

Sakatia is known for its flowers but also it has many other riches.
Nosy Sakatia is only 6 km long and 2 wide .
it is the most authentic island of archipelago Nosy Be (protected and classified by the Ministry of Environment).
The inland is pretty hilly and it offers beautiful hikes, especially at higher elevations.
Experience on an island recognized and protected as a Nature Reserve.
Sea and sun punctuate the culture and traditional activities of the inhabitants: fishermen, artisans, children and the elderly are immersed
in everyday life to the rhythm of an astonishing nature.
The coral reef is easily reached by swimming.
The island is world famous for its natural pools located under the Sacred Mountain.
The depths rich in poseidonia are the ideal habitat
for giant sea turtles: these turtles are so accustomed to the harmless movement of the fishermen with their motorless pirogues
that the human presence has become familiar. You will swim very close to them.
