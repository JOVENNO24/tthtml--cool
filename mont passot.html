description:

Mont Passot is a mountain which reaches a height of 329 meters, the highest point of Nosy Be, it is surrounded by small blue lakes considered sacred. Located on this tropical island of Madagascar, Mont Passot is a must-see destination for hiking enthusiasts and nature lovers. With its panoramic summit offering breathtaking views of the island and its surroundings, this climb promises a memorable experience, featuring among the hikes to do during your stay in Nosy Be.
