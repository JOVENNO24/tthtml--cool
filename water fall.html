WATER FALL

description:

It's also one of the best places to see Nosy Be's crater lakes.
You should not miss a visit to the nice waterfall of Androadroatra with its crystal-clear opal waters.
A 50m cascade sliding and falling over a wet rock wall all covered with moss and lichen into a natural pool.
It is important to note that the site is considered sacred by its location.
It is a place of worship for the Sakalava.
Also, even if swimming is allowed, it's important to learn about the taboos before making oneself comfortable
because to oppose them would be, according to the natives, a cause for a curse.
